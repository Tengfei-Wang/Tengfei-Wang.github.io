Tengfei WANG

I am a third-year Ph.D. student at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), advised by Qifeng Chen. I was a research intern at Tencent AI Lab from May to December, 2021. I am currently a research intern at Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA).
I'm interested in image/video processing, image/video generation, and other low-level/physically-based vision topics.
