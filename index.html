Tengfei WANG

I am a second-year Ph.D. student at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), advised by Qifeng Chen. Prior to HKUST, I got my Bachelor's degree at Beihang University in 2019. I am now a research intern at Computer Vision Center, Tencent AI Lab.
I'm interested in image/video processing, image/video generation, and other low-level/physically-based vision topics.
